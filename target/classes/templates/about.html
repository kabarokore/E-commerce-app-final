Our Story

The Daily Grind was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to share our passion for exceptional coffee with the world. What started as a small caf√© in downtown has grown into a community of coffee enthusiasts and a home for those who appreciate the art and science behind the perfect cup.

Our journey began when our founder, Emma Bennett, returned from traveling across the major coffee regions of the world. Inspired by the diverse flavors and brewing methods she encountered, Emma wanted to create a space where people could experience coffee in its purest form while learning about its origins and production.

Today, The Daily Grind stands as a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and the joy of sharing great coffee.

Our Values

Sustainability

We source our beans through direct trade relationships with farmers who practice sustainable agriculture and receive fair compensation for their work.

Quality

We meticulously select, roast, and brew our coffee to ensure that every cup meets our exacting standards of excellence.

Community

We believe coffee brings people together. Our spaces are designed to foster connection and conversation.

Education

We're committed to sharing our knowledge about coffee, from bean to cup, empowering customers to brew perfectly at home.
